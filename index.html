About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington. My research is focused on model development for compositional data applied to the microbiome, advised by Daniela Witten and Amy Willis. My research is supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Before coming to UW, I was an undergraduate at Macalester College where I received degrees in statistics and economics.
