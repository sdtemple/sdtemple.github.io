About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington. I am interested in modeling biological systems as stochastic processes. This interest motivates my participation in the UW Statistical Genetics Seminar.

Before coming to UW, I provided actuarial services to Liberty Mutual. I completed my undergraduate studies at University of Oregon where I majored in mathematics and minored in computer and information sciences.
