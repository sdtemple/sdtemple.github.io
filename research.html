Occupancy Modeling

At Los Alamos National Laboratory, I developed a fast Gibbs sampler for Bayesian logistic and probit regression in occupancy modeling. Dr. Kimberly Kaufeld and I plan to publish the corresponding paper and software package in 2021. I also consulted on a project modeling suitable ecological niches for mosquitos important to West Nile virus transmission.


Tweedie Distributions

I wrote an undergraduate honors thesis with Chris Sinclair. This paper introduces advanced undergraduate and actuarial audiences to a subclass of exponential families.


Natural Language Processing

I worked as an undergraduate research assistant for Stephen Fickas on predicting punctuation for text.
